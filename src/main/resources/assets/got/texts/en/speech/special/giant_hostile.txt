Hangalttaj! Bi chinij bүh yasyg hugalahaas өmnө chi daraagijn ertөncөd ochih cag bolloo!
Ta үnekheer yaazh tulaldahaa medekhgүj bajna, #! Bi chamajg nүcgen garaaraa mushgizh, tolgojg chin' hugalah bolno!
Tijm ee, bi chamajg agshin zuur hanan deegүүr tүrhekh bolno, tegvel eezh chin' hүrtel chamajg tanihgүj, za yuu?!
Chi nadaas zugtazh chadna gezh bodozh bajna uu? Yamar ch bajsan chamajg hөөzh, duusgana gezh najdah hereggүj!
Haraach, ter end huvcaslasan bajna! Tany өөdөs tand tusalna gezh bodozh bajna uu? Bi tednijg chamaas urzh, gishgene!
Ingeed l chinij tөgsgөl irlee, #! Өvөg deedestejgee uulzahad belen bajgaaraj!
Za, boloo, hangalttaj chalchaa! Chi үhegsdijn egneend negdekh cag n' bolson, ojlgozh bajna uu?!
Udahgүj tolgoj chin' mөrөn deerees chin' nisne, ojlgozh bajna uu?! Bi chinij tөlөө үүnijg өөrijn garaar urzh hayah bolno!
Al' hedijn dogdolhoo bol'! Bi chinij gavlyn yasyg hugalahaas өmnө өvөg deedes deeree ochih cag bolson!
